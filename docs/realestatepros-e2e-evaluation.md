## Current workforce state

- Workspace purpose saved: sales growth for a Canterbury residential sales, lettings, and investor-opportunity business.
- First room created: **Sales Outreach** with the suggested Prospecting, Sequences, and Follow-ups topics.
- Maya exists and the hiring flow was started.
- **No new AI employee was hired on production**: the live flow was blocked before the job brief/applicant stage.
- A separate local retry completed onboarding, retained the custom **Lettings Growth Manager** title, and reached the recruiter’s next question. It was intentionally not taken through candidate selection/hiring because the user’s production workspace has not received this code yet.

## Priority actions

1. Deploy the implemented timeout/recovery and custom-title fixes, then repeat the production hire through candidate selection.
2. Make onboarding/hiring domain-aware and demonstrate that the full business context survives into the role recommendation and job brief.
3. Add operational transparency to AI/recruiting work: elapsed time, current stage, retry, and a plain-language failure message.
